Animal Recognition


Crop damage due to animal intrusion is a critical problem faced by farmers in many parts of India, especially in areas near national parks and wildlife sanctuaries. It is not possible to manually monitor agricultural lands 24x7. In this paper, we have designed a farm protection system and implemented it in a small scale. The objective of this system is to protect the crops from animals without harming them. It alerts people about the intrusion by wild animals thereby reducing the potential risk to human life. To distinguish between domestic and wild animals, image of the intruder is captured and classified using a IBM Visual Recognition Service, which is trained for recognizing animals that are a predominant threat to crops. The system is flexible i.e. it can be easily modified to identify any animal.
